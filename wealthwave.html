WEALTHWAVE "savings made easy"

The lack of existing money saving apps is worrying, People are wanting something that helps them save money for those big things without all of the stress.

My process

I started with researching competitive apps to see what they offered and didnt offer. I indentified the problems users were having with these apps thorugh reserach. Then, I came up with ideas of what would work best for users. I designed and tested and finally created final mockups.

research

I conducted user research and user surveys

ideation

This process consisted of finding inspiration, sketching crazy 8's, finding a color pallete, and getting the overall feel for what I wanted my app to look like.

Wireframes

I sketched basic wireframes and then created them on Figma

design

This process was my favorite. I implimented my colors and UI elements and brought it to life.
